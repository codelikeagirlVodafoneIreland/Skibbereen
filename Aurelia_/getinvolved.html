Volunteering

The Aurelia Trust runs tabaras(summer camps) for the young adults every summer. An average day would contain the following:

A typical day at the beach

  1. 7.00am wake up & get ready
  2. 7.20am breakfast
  3. 8.00am bus leaves to go to the beach
  4. 8.40am-11.30am Beach - swimming, playing games,building sand castles, etc.
  5. 11.45am- 2.00pm Lunch, free to rest for a few hours
  6. 2.15pm bus leaves to go back to the beach
  7. 2.40pm- 5pm Beach as above
  • Evening free to go into the local town(Eforie) or usually go back to Techirghiol - eat in local restaurants most evenings
  • A day at the pilot centre would usually start later (approx. 10am) - quiet intense at times less hours
