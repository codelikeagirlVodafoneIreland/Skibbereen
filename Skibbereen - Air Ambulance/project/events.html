They have had many events including a 24hr tractor run, spike island After Dark Tour, The Fearghal Beamish Memorial walk and run, RMS Lusitania and modern Safety at Sea, Easter Egg Hunt, Charity Channel pool swim and many more. People have donated very generously. Flag days and donation buckets have also largly contributed to the development of the air ambulance. The amount of money we aim to raise is 1 Million euro.
