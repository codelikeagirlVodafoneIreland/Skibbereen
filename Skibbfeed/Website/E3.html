12 THINGS YOU ONLY KNOW IF YOU’RE WAYYYY TOO OBSESSED WITH CHRISTMAS

In every friendship group there's always that one person who completely and utterly froths for Christmas. They're the one who sends you invites to every single festive event in your city and insists on having a proper Christmas lunch for the squad. If you don't have a friend like that, it's because you are that friend. Here are 12 things you only know if you're hopelessly OBSESSED with everything to do with December 25.

1. First thing's first, Christmas is not a day, it’s a way of life.




2. Your holiday prep work starts on November 1, the day after Halloween.




3. Everyone else rolls their eyes when shopping centres bring out their Christmas decorations in November. Little do they know your crib is already decked AF.




4. You have seen Love Actually approx. five zillion times.




5. And Elf is your festive spirit animal.




6. You have a Christmas countdown app on your phone, starting on Boxing Day.




7. You will wear a tacky Christmas sweater even when it’s 40 degrees outside because you’re that dedicated.




8. Your pet also legit hates you because you dress ‘em up like a mini Santa every single year. But you have no ragrats.




9. You’re that one who actually puts these reindeer ears on your car.




10. You can’t just ‘buy anything’ for your loved one at Christmas. Na ah. It has to be PERFECT.




11. But you secretly live for the mania that is Christmas Eve shopping.




12. When people say they don’t like Christmas you can’t help but get angry.
