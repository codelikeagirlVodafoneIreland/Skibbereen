Feel Good Films

“Frida” -
This is the true story of Frida Kahlo (Salma Hayek) and her husband
Diego Rivera (Alfred Molina) who became the most famous artists in Mexico. It is a good film to express your femininity, creativity and sexuality.


“The Devil Wears Prada”
This is a story about a young girl called Andy (Anne Hathaway) who has big dreams for her life.
After getting a job at an important runway magazine, she becomes the assistant to diabolical Miranda Priestly (Meryl Streep).


“Mama Mia” -
This musical follows the fun story of Donna (Meryl Streep), a single mother in Greece and her daughter Sophie’s (Amanda Seyfried) wedding.
Sophie invites three of her potential fathers to her wedding hoping to find her father whom she has never met. This film is famous for its “Abba” soundtrack that will certainly make you sing along.


“Mulan”
This Disney classic follows the story of a young woman, Mulan, who takes her fathers place in the Chinese military by dressing as a man. This is a heartwarming film of self discovery and bravery suitable for all the family.


“Chocolate”
When mysterious Vianne (Juliette Binoche) and her child arrive in a tranquil French town in the winter of 1959,
no one could have imagined the impact that she and her spirited daughter would have on the community stubbornly rooted in tradition. She opens an unusual chocolate shop during lent and she faces the disapproval of the small town.


“The Danish Girl”
Based on the 2000 fictional novel of the same name by David Ebershoff, and loosely inspired by the lives of Danish painters Lili Elbe (Eddie Redmayne) and Gerda Wegener (Alicia Vikander). When Gerda Wegener’s husband begins to feel more feminine and tries on Gerda’s clothes as a joke,
he begins to feel as if he should be a woman. This is an inspirational story of a young transgender woman who isn’t accepted by the society she lives in.


“She’s the Man”
When her brother decides to ditch for a couple weeks, Viola (Amanda Bynes) heads over to his elite boarding school,
disguised as him, and proceeds to fall for one of his soccer teammates, and soon learns she's not the only one with romantic troubles.
